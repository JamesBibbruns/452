Ninghai beckons diverse birdlife as spring approaches

With the arrival of spring, the Baixi waters, nestled in Chalu town, Ningbo's Ninghai county, have transformed into a sanctuary for a diverse range of avian species, creating a stunning portrait of life in the area. Renowned for its breathtaking natural vistas and rich ecological wealth, Chalu has garnered national recognition as an ecological town. Within this picturesque locale, the Baixi waters have evolved into an optimal haven for a variety of waterfowls, including ducks, herons, and cormorants.

Unmanned delivery vehicle fleet debuts in Ningbo

A fleet of seven unmanned delivery vehicles made its debut on the streets of Ningbo, Zhejiang province on March 13, marking a significant step forward in the integration of autonomous technology in the logistics industry. The vehicles, featuring a pristine white finish and prominently displaying the insignia of SF Express, a leading courier company in China, each have a cargo capacity of 3 cubic meters and a maximum payload of 500 kilograms. They are capable of reaching a top speed of 50 kilometers per hour and can cover a range of 200 km on a full charge.
